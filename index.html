What It Does

Every food decision,
guided before you make it.

Six tools designed to put you in control — at the restaurant, in the kitchen, and everywhere in between.

📸

Menu Scanner

Photo a restaurant menu and get instant AI rankings of the safest dishes for your glucose — plus what to ask your server to make any dish more diabetes-friendly.

🍳

Cook Smart

Tell us what's in your fridge and get low-glycemic recipes built around your ingredients, with clear explanations of the glucose impact of each dish.

💬

Ask Anything

A food-focused AI companion that answers questions like "Is brown rice okay for me?" or "What's a good breakfast before my morning walk?" in plain language.

🛒

Grocery Guide

Scan a nutrition label or search any food to understand exactly how it affects blood sugar. Build smarter shopping lists with confidence.

✈️

Travel Mode

Traveling abroad? Get food guidance tailored to local cuisines — from Turkish breakfast to Japanese izakaya — with culturally accurate recommendations.

📋

Doctor Prep

Generate a clear, organized summary of your eating patterns and food choices to share with your endocrinologist or dietitian at your next visit.

How It Works

From menu to
decision in seconds.

Open DiaWise the moment you need it. No complicated setup. No learning curve.

1

Show DiaWise the menu

Take a photo of a restaurant menu, upload a recipe, or simply describe what you're considering eating. DiaWise understands context.

2

Get ranked recommendations

DiaWise analyzes every dish and ranks your options — safest first — with a plain-language explanation of the glucose impact of each choice.

3

Order with confidence

Get specific server questions to ask, portion guidance, and substitution suggestions that turn any restaurant into a diabetes-friendly experience.

Fasting Mode

Built for Ramadan.
Built for every fast.

Managing diabetes during Ramadan is one of the most complex food challenges a person can face. DiaWise's Fasting Mode was designed with this in mind — and works for intermittent fasting and pre-lab fasting too.

Get Suhoor and Iftar meal plans optimized for stable glucose across the fasting hours, with safe foods to break the fast and what to avoid when hunger peaks.
